Help with adding a product

Your progress in completing each task is saved as a working draft until you are ready to accept and submit it. Product drafts are stored in the Cosmetic products page from where you can resume and complete the remaining tasks at any time.

Before you accept and submit a draft you can change information at any stage by completing individual tasks again. If you have added items (for multi-item kits) or nanomaterials, you can add or remove them individually at any stage.
