UK Responsible Person details

Help with the Responsible Person details

A Responsible Person can be a business, in the law this is called a 'legal person'. A Responsible Person can also be a living person, in the law this is called a 'natural person'.

The Responsible Person must be a legal entity in the UK.

The Responsible Person must provide a UK address.

A Responsible Person is legally bound by the obligations in the Cosmetics Regulation.

! Warning The UK address provided must not be a PO box or mail forwarding address. These are not UK established addresses that comply with the regulation.
